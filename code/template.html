What is Bionomia?

Members of the public log into Bionomia, look at a specimen record which is missing a person ID, and explicitly specify who the collector is by adding an ORCID or Wikidata QID.

For example, a specimen where the collector is recorded simply as "H. Printz" can be linked to H. Printz, so we know this specimen was collected by Henrik Printz born 1888 (https://bionomia.net/Q21313999), and not Hans Printz born 1817 (https://bionomia.net/Q15091583).

Sometimes, collection management systems (e.g. MUSIT or Corema) are missing these explicit links to IDs, and it's useful to import them in from Bionomia. We can help you with this process. The first step is to review the attributions added in Bionomia and see if there are any you think are incorrect, then contact helpdesk@gbif.no.
